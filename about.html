About Us

Our Journey

At Gentler Coparent, we understand the emotional and practical challenges of high-conflict coparenting because we’ve been there. Our founder, a coparent who navigated the complexities of a high-conflict divorce, experienced firsthand the toll that hostile communication can take on both parents and children.

The Problem

High-conflict coparenting often leads to emotionally charged exchanges that escalate tensions, create stress, and negatively impact the well-being of everyone involved—especially the children. Traditional communication methods can fall short, leaving coparents feeling frustrated, misunderstood, or stuck in cycles of conflict.

The Solution

That’s why we created Gentler Coparent: an AI-powered tool designed to help coparents communicate with clarity, empathy, and respect. Our tool analyzes your messages and rephrases them to reduce hostility, foster cooperation, and maintain a focus on what matters most—your children’s well-being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform coparenting communication by providing a tool that empowers parents to break free from conflict and build a more peaceful, cooperative relationship. We believe that better communication leads to better outcomes for families, and we’re here to support you every step of the way.
